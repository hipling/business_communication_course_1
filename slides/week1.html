Shadowing Technique Overview

What is Shadowing?

  • Repeating after a native speaker to mimic pronunciation, rhythm, and intonation
  • Boosts fluency, listening, and confidence

Why for Business?

  • Enhances clarity in calls, presentations, and meetings
16 / 25
3 minutes

Benefits of Shadowing

Key Advantages:

  • Improves pronunciation accuracy
  • Builds natural speech patterns
  • Enhances listening comprehension
  • Increases confidence in real-time conversations

Example: Shadowing a TED Talk improves pitch delivery

17 / 25
3 minutes

Shadowing Technique Steps

Steps:

  • Select: A short, clear audio (e.g., business podcast or speech)
  • Listen: Once to understand context
  • Repeat: Simultaneously, mimicking tone and pace
  • Record: Yourself and compare to the original
  • Practice: 2-3 times for improvement
